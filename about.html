About Calipr
We're a team of passionate technologists dedicated to transforming businesses through innovative IT solutions.
Our Story

Founded in 2024, Calipr emerged from a vision to bridge the gap between cutting-edge technology and practical business solutions. Our founders, with decades of combined experience in software development and digital transformation, recognized that many businesses were struggling to keep pace with rapid technological advancements.

What began as a small team of dedicated developers has grown into a comprehensive IT solutions provider, serving clients across various industries. Our journey has been defined by a relentless pursuit of excellence, continuous learning, and a commitment to delivering exceptional value to our clients.

Today, Calipr stands at the forefront of the IT revolution, helping businesses of all sizes harness the power of technology to achieve their goals, streamline their operations, and create meaningful connections with their customers.
