How long does it take to develop a mobile app?

The timeline varies depending on the complexity and scope of the project. A basic app typically takes 3-4 months, while more complex apps can take 6-12 months or more. We'll provide a detailed timeline during the project planning phase.

Should I build a native app or a cross-platform app?

It depends on your specific needs. Native apps offer the best performance and access to platform-specific features but require separate development for iOS and Android. Cross-platform apps are more cost-effective and faster to develop but may have some limitations. We'll help you choose the best approach based on your requirements and budget.

How do you ensure the quality of the app?

We follow a rigorous quality assurance process that includes functional testing, usability testing, performance testing, security testing, and compatibility testing across different devices and operating system versions.

Do you help with app store submission?

Yes, we handle the entire app store submission process for both Apple App Store and Google Play Store, including preparing all necessary assets, descriptions, and metadata.

Do you provide app maintenance services?

Yes, we offer app maintenance packages that include regular updates, bug fixes, performance optimization, and feature enhancements. This ensures your app remains compatible with the latest operating systems and devices.

Can you add features to my existing app?

Yes, we can enhance your existing app with new features and functionality. We'll analyze your current app architecture and code to determine the best approach for implementing the new features.
