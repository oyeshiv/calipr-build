Frequently Asked Questions
Find answers to common questions about our website development services.

How long does it take to build a website?

The timeline varies depending on the complexity and scope of the project. A basic website typically takes 4-6 weeks, while more complex websites can take 8-12 weeks or more. We'll provide a detailed timeline during the project planning phase.

Do you provide website hosting?

Yes, we offer website hosting services with various packages to suit your needs. Our hosting includes regular backups, security monitoring, and technical support.

Can you redesign my existing website?

We specialize in website redesigns that improve both aesthetics and functionality. We'll analyze your current website, identify areas for improvement, and create a modern, user-friendly design.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance?

Yes, we offer website maintenance packages that include regular updates, security patches, performance optimization, and content updates. This ensures your website remains secure, up-to-date, and performing optimally.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Yes, all our websites are built with a mobile-first approach, ensuring they look and function perfectly on all devices, from desktops to smartphones and tablets.

Do you include SEO in your website development?

We implement on-page SEO best practices during development, including proper HTML structure, meta tags, schema markup, and site speed optimization. For more comprehensive SEO services, we offer dedicated SEO packages.
