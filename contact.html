Frequently Asked Questions
Find answers to common questions about our services and process.

What services does Calipr offer?

Calipr offers a comprehensive range of IT services including website development, mobile app development, CRM integration, web app development, UI/UX design, and quality assurance testing.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. A simple website might take 4–6 weeks, while a complex web application could take 3–6 months.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?

Yes, we offer various support and maintenance packages to ensure your digital products continue to perform optimally.

What is your development process?

We follow an agile development methodology: discovery, planning, design, development, testing, deployment, and support.

How do you handle project pricing?

We offer both fixed-price and time-and-materials models, depending on your needs and project complexity.

Can you work with our existing systems and technologies?

Yes, we specialize in integrating with various third-party systems, APIs, and legacy tech stacks.
